Frequently asked questions

What is Onyx and how does it support educators?
Onyx is an AI-powered content generator designed to help teachers create culturally responsive, standards-aligned ELA and Math lessons rooted in Black history and culture—quickly and effectively.
Who can use Onyx?
Onyx is built for K–12 educators, curriculum designers, and school leaders who want to enrich their classrooms with meaningful, inclusive content.
What subjects and grade levels does Onyx support?
Currently, Onyx supports ELA and Math for grades K–12, with more subjects and grade levels coming soon.
How long does it take to generate a lesson?
Onyx typically takes 3–5 minutes to generate a complete, ready-to-use lesson based on your selected topic and grade level.
What kind of topics can I create lessons about?
You can generate lessons on a wide range of topics related to Black history, culture, and excellence—including influential figures, key historical events, and culturally rich themes.
Can I create custom topics with the tool?
Yes! Onyx allows you to generate lessons based on topics you choose. Simply enter your own custom prompt or subject area, and the platform will create a culturally responsive lesson tailored to your input.
